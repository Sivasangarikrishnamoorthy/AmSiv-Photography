Hanuman

Hanuman also known as Maruti, Bajrangabali, and Anjaneya, is a deity in Hinduism, revered as a divine vanara, and a devoted companion of the deity Rama.

Siva

Shiva also known as Mahadeva is one of the principal of Hinduism. He is the Supreme Being in Shaivism, one of the major traditions within Hinduism.

Ram

Rama was born to Kausalya and Dasharatha in Ayodhya, the capital of the Kingdom of Kosala. His siblings included Lakshmana, Bharata, and Shatrughna.
